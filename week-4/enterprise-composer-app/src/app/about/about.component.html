Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was a prolific and influential composer of the classical era. He was born in 1756 in Salzburg, Austria, and showed extraordinary musical talent from an early age. His works include symphonies, operas, chamber music, and piano concertos. Mozart's music is characterized by its beauty, precision, and emotional depth. His compositions, such as "Eine kleine Nachtmusik" and "The Magic Flute," remain beloved classics in the world of classical music.
